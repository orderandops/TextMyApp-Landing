Build a modern, high-converting landing page for TextMyApp — a SaaS product that lets job applicants complete an entire job application via SMS text message. No links, no forms, no drop-off.

## Target Audience
- Construction subcontractors and trades employers (electrical, plumbing, HVAC, concrete, drywall, roofing)
- Small to mid-size companies (5-100 employees)
- HR leaders, talent acquisition, or owners who handle hiring
- Struggling to hire blue-collar workers, especially non-English speakers

## Key Messaging

### Headline
"Hire faster. Just text."

### Subheadline
"The only text-to-apply that actually completes the application—no links, no forms, no drop-off."

### The Problem (Section)
- Blue-collar workers won't complete online job applications
- Paper apps, "call to apply," and QR codes to clunky forms have massive drop-off
- Non-English speakers face language barriers on English-only job sites
- Every unfilled position costs $300-500+/day in project delays

### The Solution (Section)
Workers text a job code (e.g., "Text WELDER123 to 555-123-4567") and complete their entire application through a natural, AI-powered SMS conversation—in English or Spanish. No links to click, no forms to fill out, no app to download. Applications push directly to your applicant tracking system.

### How It Works (3 Steps)
1. **Create a Job Code** — Set up a keyword for each role (e.g., LABOR123, WELDER456)
2. **Workers Text to Apply** — They text your code and complete the application via SMS in their language
3. **Applications Land in Your ATS** — Candidate info syncs automatically—no manual entry

### Key Benefits (4 Cards or Icons)
1. **Capture More Candidates** — Reach workers who won't complete online forms
2. **Remove Language Barriers** — AI chatbot works in English and Spanish
3. **Fill Roles Faster** — Applications in minutes, not days
4. **Works With Your ATS** — Integrates with your existing applicant tracking system
